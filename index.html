Who am I?

I am Ryan Hirst, developer, designer, artist, soccer enthusiast, and amateur cook from Kalamazoo, Michigan. I Graduated from Michigan State University in May of 2015 with a degree in Media & Information, specializing in interactive media, and a minor in Computer Science.

Since then, I've immersed myself in the field of web development, finding ways to hone my skills and to push myself to learn more. I've gained experience in both designing websites and implementing the designs of others, project management with tools such as Git and Basecamp, using frameworks such as Laravel and Bootstrap, and building and customizing large scale websites using Drupal.
